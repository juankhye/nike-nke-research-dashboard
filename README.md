# Nike (NKE) — First-Principles Interactive Research Dashboard

A single-page, hedge-fund-style research dashboard for **Nike, Inc. (NYSE: NKE)**, built from a first-principles synthesis of sell-side research (UBS, Goldman Sachs, Bernstein, AlphaSense) and Nike's public FY2025 10-K.

**Live site:** see the GitHub Pages link in the repo's *About* section.

## What's inside
- **12 sections:** 60-second primer · business model · the "Win Now → Sport Offense" turnaround · geographic footprint · supply chain & tariffs · revenue/cost drivers · competitive moat · financials · valuation · bull vs bear · key risks · what to watch.
- **Two interactive maps** (jsVectorMap): revenue by reporting segment, and the contract-manufacturing footprint.
- **~15 interactive charts** (Chart.js): revenue/EPS/margins with a broker-estimate toggle (GS / UBS / Bernstein), gross-margin and category bridges, valuation scenarios, and the Street price-target scorecard.
- **Live NKE price** via a TradingView widget.

Single self-contained `index.html`, no backend.

## Disclaimer
Educational research synthesis of third-party analysis and public filings. **Not investment advice.** Figures reflect the F4Q26 print (June 30, 2026) as reported in the source research and may not reflect subsequent developments. Forecasts are the respective brokers' estimates.
